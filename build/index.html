    What do I do

    As is the case with pretty much anyone, it's kind of difficult to put into words what I do, but I'll try: I make things better. I take anything that is or isn't tangible, and add or substract from it in order to make it better.

    To be clear, I am very skilled at making ideas tangible using a wide range of software packages. However big or however small it does not matter, I can make it happen. I have worked for the biggest companies, and I have also helped the smallest players to get their feet wet.

    I have a ton of experience with all kinds of software, hardware and concepts, from Adobe to Affinity, from Atom to Sublime, from R to Excel. Mac computers, homemade Windows computers, Linux computers, none of them are any problem whatsoever. And of course, Microsoft PowerPoint and Apple Keynote too.
